Services

Our portfolio is managed by in house event specialists with qualitative wealth of experience that enables us to exceed client expectations.

  • Management & Co-ordination

    Our services include event management and coordination for clients and partners, which involves the provision of a team of usherettes and ushers to ensure that guests are well-attended to.

  • Vendor Sourcing & Selection

    For clients who do not have vendors for sub-services, we assist with Vendor Sourcing & Selection. Our service involves identifying and selecting suitable vendors who can provide the required services to ensure that the event runs smoothly.

  • Venue Decoration, Design & Styling

    We provide Venue Decoration, Design & Styling services for a variety of settings, including parlours, dining rooms, home compounds, field tents, field marquees, closed halls, and open air venues (recommended for night events during the dry season). Our expertise in this area ensures that your chosen venue is transformed into a beautiful and captivating space for your event.

  • Consultation

    We offer comprehensive event consultation services for potential clients and partners in the field. Our consultation process is thorough and detailed, ensuring that we understand all aspects of your event to provide the best possible service.
